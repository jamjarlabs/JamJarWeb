## [Unreleased]
### Added

## [0.1.0] - 2019-10-27
### Added
- Entity-Component-System architecture.
- Composition based entities and components.
- Regular game loop.
- Sprites and rendering.
- Collision detection.
- Scene management.
- Camera systems.
- Basic physics for velocities/acceleration/rotation.
